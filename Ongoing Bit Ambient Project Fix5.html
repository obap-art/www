Ongoing bit ambient project, obap, has been invented in 2016. Some of the material is however far from the past. Material can also be found in a variety of side projects for a decade. obap is a style, a grand style and kind of way to present

One of the main idea is to make visible and interesting form any essential or hidden aspects of recorded space. We are constantly creating, discovering, and testing innovative ways to produce both audio and video content. By combining audio and video in new and exciting ways, we are able to create unique experiences that captivate.

Everything we experience already exists, and by taking a moment to stop and immerse ourselves in these experiences, we can achieve a sense of integrative relaxation and empowerment. Just like being in nature and observing and just watching it, can be at its best

While it may not always be feasible to physically stop and experience something firsthand, we can still engage with it through a screen. Although nothing can truly replace the authenticity of being present in the moment, viewing something from a different perspective can reveal unexpected and extraordinary elements which could not be expected to happen when it was actually happening.

USER QUIDE

The obap web page has a variety of experience related features. You can enlarge or reduce the image to suit your size. Set repeat feature to continuous, random, single viewing and playall (button in main page) that keeps playin art works randomly forever. There is also a sleeptimer button on player controller that darkens the screen and the sound evaporates evenly after the desired time, so that it doesn´t break the achieved atmosphere or unity.

IDEA

Here is one of idea to produce audio, music. The sound landscape is 100% recorded from the shooting location and after which the audio track is mixed and controlled through the midi translator to create digital notes and play information. This information is guided to various virtual synthesizers, samplers. From this coming sound stage, the music has been mixed once and finally mastered.

This way allow us to think that the natural sound of a environment and space is a musician and composer of music. You can find interesting visuals, motion, rhytms, melodies, patterns and soundscapes from the nature.

SOME RELEASES:

KANSI

Photos, videos and chroma keyed videos are projected into three-dimensional compositions within the internal space in computer, after which they are illuminated and filmed a again with their software cameras (matte painting) and color defined, mastered. Videos have also been manipulated with the expression script from audio tracks.

Material has been recorded and filmed in three diffrent location in Finland, Lahti area.

NOBA

Natures own bit ambient (NOBA) audio release is fully produced using the idea as mentoned upow (idea graff). Natural sound of a environment and space is a musician and composer of music. The work should be approached like painting on the wall. The idea is passive experience. You can find interesting visuals, motion and soundscapes from the nature. Part of the composition of the video is to make video cropping and cutting as an still photograph. Nature can play itself.

AMBSU

Modular modeling of synthesizers. In these release, man directs digital music notes to the instruments. through various virtual module controls, the synthesizers form part of the random audio. Some audio tracks have been converted to keyframes and this information is directed to internal software camera movements, as well as video effects.

Location of lahti center area, with the exception of a intro song which material has been filmed on an island in Lake Vesijärvi and outro is filmed in river call oravan route. Some videos are produced similar way as Kansi release.
